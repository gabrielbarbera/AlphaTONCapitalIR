Brittany Kaiser

Chief Executive Officer, Board Member

Brittany Kaiser serves as Chief Executive Officer and Board Member of AlphaTON Capital Corp. She is a policy and technology strategist with extensive experience advising governments and enterprises on regulatory innovation in the digital asset space.

Prior to joining AlphaTON Capital, Brittany held senior positions at leading technology companies and government agencies, where she developed expertise in blockchain regulation, digital asset compliance, and institutional adoption strategies.

Brittany holds a Juris Doctor from Harvard Law School and a Bachelor of Arts in Political Science from Stanford University. She is a recognized expert in digital asset regulation and has been featured in numerous publications and conferences on the intersection of technology and policy.

Key Achievements

  • Led regulatory strategy for multiple digital asset companies
  • Advised government agencies on blockchain policy frameworks
  • Published extensively on digital asset regulation
  • Speaker at major industry conferences and events

Enzo Villani

Chief Investment Officer, Board Member

Enzo Villani serves as Chief Investment Officer and Board Member of AlphaTON Capital Corp. He is the founder of Alpha Transform Holdings and brings over 15 years of experience as a veteran strategist bridging public markets and digital assets.

Enzo has been instrumental in developing AlphaTON Capital's investment strategy, focusing on high-performance infrastructure investments in the TON ecosystem. His expertise spans traditional finance, digital asset markets, and blockchain infrastructure.

Prior to founding Alpha Transform Holdings, Enzo held senior investment positions at leading financial institutions, where he managed institutional portfolios and developed innovative investment strategies for emerging asset classes.

Key Achievements

  • Founded Alpha Transform Holdings
  • Managed over $2B in institutional assets
  • Pioneered digital asset investment strategies
  • Led successful exits in blockchain infrastructure

Yury Mitin

Partner, Chief Business Development Officer

Yury Mitin serves as Partner and Chief Business Development Officer of AlphaTON Capital Corp. He is a Managing Partner at RSV Capital and specializes in late-stage growth investment strategies with extensive experience in emerging technology markets.

Yury brings deep expertise in ecosystem development and strategic partnerships, having led business development initiatives for multiple technology companies in the blockchain and fintech sectors. His focus is on building strategic relationships that drive growth and value creation.

Prior to joining AlphaTON Capital, Yury held senior business development roles at leading technology companies, where he established partnerships with major corporations and financial institutions.

Key Achievements

  • Managing Partner at RSV Capital
  • Led strategic partnerships worth over $500M
  • Expertise in late-stage growth investments
  • Established key ecosystem partnerships
